Overview

Nova at Summer Meadows

Located at 433 Hebron Road in Durham, NC, Nova at Summer Meadows is a luxury apartment community on approximately 5.47 acres. Offering a selection of one, two, and three-bedroom apartments, this development combines style and functionality with high-end finishes and modern conveniences. The community caters to a young, dynamic demographic, with over a third of the metro area's population aged between 20 and 34, making it a vibrant and desirable place to live.

Residents of Nova at Summer Meadows enjoy spacious floor plans and a range of curated amenities that enhance both relaxation and social activities. The property's proximity to North Duke Shopping Mall, Duke Regional Hospital, and key locations like Downtown Durham and Duke University—just five miles away—places residents within easy reach of premium shopping, dining, and healthcare services.

The upcoming Apple campus, less than 10 miles away in the Research Triangle Park, adds to the area's appeal, promising to draw a technology-oriented workforce and boost rental demand. Durham's growth outpaces national averages, driven by a stable and diverse economy, with significant developments in healthcare, education, and technology sectors enhancing its nvestment potential​. Source: REBusinessOnline & CWS Capital

Nova at Summer Meadows represents a prime opportunity to invest in a region experiencing rapid growth and demographic expansion, making it an attractive option for those looking to capitalize on Durham's promising future.
